## K Prop Source Change
- **Removed PrizePicks** — generic lines (often 4.5 for everyone), 0-5 record on K props over the weekend
- **Removed Underdog Fantasy** — DFS platform, not a real sportsbook, lines differ from DK
- **Added DraftKings lines via BettingPros API** — `fetch_draftkings_k_props()` in odds.py
  - Endpoint: `api.bettingpros.com/v3/props?sport=MLB&market_id=285&book_id=12` (12=DraftKings)
  - Free, no auth, returns real DK lines with American odds (juice)
  - No rate limiting issues at 1 call/day

## Alt Line Logic
- When over juice is worse than -110, recommends flat lower number
- Example: "OVER 6.5 at -124" becomes "OVER 6 Ks (juice on 6.5 is -124)"
- Only fires for OVER picks with bad juice

## Game Predictor Fixes
- **Market blend**: Fixed hardcoded 50/50 to use config's 60/40 market/model — was ignoring MARKET_WEIGHT/MODEL_WEIGHT from config.py
- **Confidence scoring**: Reduced data availability max from 75 to 45. Signal agreement now drives confidence (max 45). Model+market agree = +18 bonus. Contradiction penalty raised from -8 to -12
- **Underdog flip**: Requires 52% model conviction (was 48% — basically a coin flip)
- **Result**: Rangers picks that lost twice at 87 confidence would now be ~55 LEAN

## Real Book Whitelist
- Only these bookmakers qualify for market blending in strikeout_predictor.py: draftkings, fanduel, betmgm, caesars, pointsbet, betrivers
- PrizePicks, Underdog, and unknown sources skip market blending
